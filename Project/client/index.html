DEVIL DEALER ○ HELP
× What Is Devil Dealer?

Devil dealer is a party drinking game where players take turns guessing the card in the dealers hand (2-14 with ace high). The guesser gets two guesses- after the first guess they are told if the dealer's card is higher or lower. If the card is correctly guessed the dealer must drink their beverage, and if the card is not guessed the guesser must drink. The trick to this game however is that after a card is played it is displayed on the table for all to see, thus narrowing down the amount of cards to guess. Towards the end of the game the dealer role becomes a losing battle as it gets very easy to guess the card.


Drink Distribution

If the dealer's card is guessed first time: the dealer drinks the number of the card in sips

If the dealer's card is guessed second time: the dealer drinks the difference between the two guesses

If the guesser does not guess the card: the guesser drinks the difference between their second guess and the dealers card
